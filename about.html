About Me

Hi, I'm Laura.

Hi, I'm Laura, founder of Dymond Bookkeeping. With over 20 years' experience in bookkeeping and finance, I help owner-led businesses stay organised, compliant and confident in their numbers.

I believe bookkeeping should feel straightforward, supportive and stress-free, giving you more time to focus on running your business.

Outside of bookkeeping, I'm a wife and mum who understands first-hand the demands of juggling family life and running a business. That's why I believe bookkeeping should reduce stress rather than add to it. My aim is to build long-term relationships with my clients, providing reliable support throughout the year, not just when deadlines are approaching.
