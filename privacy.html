Privacy Policy

At Dymond Bookkeeping, I am committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly.

Dymond Bookkeeping provides bookkeeping and related financial administration services to businesses across the UK.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: hello@dymondbookkeeping.com.

If you contact me by email or through the contact form, I may collect:

  • Your name
  • Business name
  • Email address

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used to:
